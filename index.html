About QUNATUM 2K23

Quantum is a National Level Technical Symposium for undergrads organised by Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Sri Venkateswara Institute Of Technology Ananthapuramu. The fest is a high profile event which will lure the students of all colleges in and around the country to uncover their all-round expertise by competing emulously on a large scale that paves the way for all-round development of a budding professional.
